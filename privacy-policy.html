Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. This page explains how MindFlux AI handles information responsibly.

Introduction

At MindFlux AI, protecting user privacy is a core priority. This Privacy Policy document explains how information is handled when you visit or interact with our website.

MindFlux AI is designed to provide informational and educational AI tools. We believe users should clearly understand how their data is treated, without confusion or hidden practices.

Information We Do Not Collect

MindFlux AI does not require users to create accounts, log in, or provide personal identification details to access most of our tools.

We do not intentionally collect:

  • Names, addresses, or phone numbers
  • Financial or payment information
  • Government identification data
  • Private credentials or passwords

Our tools are built to function without storing sensitive personal data.

Some tools on MindFlux AI may allow users to enter text, such as descriptions, messages, or general inputs, for the purpose of generating insights.

These inputs are processed temporarily and are not permanently stored. They are used only to deliver the requested functionality.

Users are encouraged not to submit sensitive or confidential information.

Any information processed on MindFlux AI is used solely to:

We do not sell, rent, or trade user information to third parties.
